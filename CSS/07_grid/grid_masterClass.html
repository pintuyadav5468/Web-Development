CSS Grid Masterclass

Drag the sliders, flip the dropdowns — watch the grid and the code change together.

1 · Grid Basics — Columns & Rows

A grid is a parent with display:grid. You define tracks (columns/rows), and children flow into the cells.


                                                
Key idea: repeat(3, 1fr) means "3
                                                            columns, each taking 1 fraction of the free space."
                                                            fr is the grid superpower — it splits leftover
                                                            room automatically.

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
2 · Track Sizing — fr, px, auto, minmax

                                                
Columns don't have to be equal. Mix fixed and flexible units. Click a
                                                            preset to see the difference.

                                    

                                    

                                                

                                                

                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                
minmax(100px, 1fr) = "never shrink below 100px,
                                                            but grow to fill space." This is the secret to responsive
                                                            grids without media queries.

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
3 · Spanning Items — grid-column & grid-row

                                                
Any item can stretch across multiple tracks. Click an item to make it
                                                            span 2 columns; the orange item is controlled by the
                                                            sliders.

                                    

                                    

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                
grid-column: span 2 tells one item to occupy
                                                            two column tracks. You can also use exact lines:
                                                            grid-column: 1 / 3 (from line 1 to line 3).
                                                

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
4 · Alignment — justify & align

                                                
Two axes: justify-* = horizontal (inline),
                                                            align-* = vertical (block).
                                                            *-items positions content inside cells;
                                                            *-content positions the whole grid in extra
                                                            space.

                                    

                                    

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
5 · Responsive Grid — auto-fit & auto-fill

                                                
The famous one-liner. Resize the container with the slider and watch
                                                            columns wrap automatically — no media queries needed.

                                    

                                    

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        

                                                            

                                                

                                                

                                                
auto-fit vs auto-fill: when items don't fill
                                                            the row, auto-fit stretches them to absorb the
                                                            empty tracks, while auto-fill keeps the empty
                                                            tracks (items stay their min size). Shrink the width and add
                                                            items to feel the difference.

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
6 · Named Areas — grid-template-areas

                                                
Draw your layout as ASCII art. Each name maps to an item via
                                                            grid-area. Best for page layouts. Toggle the
                                                            mobile layout.

Why it's great: rearranging the whole page is
                                                            as simple as rewriting the grid-template-areas
                                                            string. The HTML never changes.

                                    

                        


                        
                        

                                    

                                                
7 · Quick Reference Cheatsheet

                                                
The properties you'll reach for 90% of the time.

                                    

                                    

                                                
On the container (parent)

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        Property
                                                                        What it does
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        display: grid
                                                                        Turns the element into a grid container.
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-template-columns
                                                                        Defines the column tracks, e.g.
                                                                                    1fr 2fr or
                                                                                    repeat(3, 1fr).
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-template-rows
                                                                        Defines the row tracks.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        gap
                                                                        Space between tracks (shorthand for
                                                                                    row-gap column-gap).
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-template-areas
                                                                        Names regions to place items by name.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        justify-items / align-items
                                                                        
                                                                        Align cell content horizontally /
                                                                                    vertically.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        justify-content / align-content
                                                                        
                                                                        Align the whole grid within leftover space.
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-auto-flow
                                                                        row | column |
                                                                                    dense — how auto-placed
                                                                                    items fill gaps.
                                                            

                                                

                                                
On the items (children)

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        Property
                                                                        What it does
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-column
                                                                        span 2 or 1 / 3 —
                                                                                    which columns the item occupies.
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-row
                                                                        Same, for rows.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        grid-area
                                                                        Assign to a named area, or
                                                                                    row-start / col-start / row-end / col-end.
                                                                        
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        justify-self / align-self
                                                                        Override alignment for a single item.
                                                            

                                                

                                                
Handy units & functions

                                                

                                                            

                                                                        Token
                                                                        Meaning
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        fr
                                                                        A fraction of the remaining free space.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        repeat(n, …)
                                                                        Repeat a track pattern n times.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        minmax(min, max)
                                                                        Track stays between a min and max size.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        auto-fit / auto-fill
                                                                        Auto-create as many tracks as fit — the
                                                                                    responsive trick.
                                                            

                                                            

                                                                        min-content / max-content
                                                                        Size to the smallest / largest content.
